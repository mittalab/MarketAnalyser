Core Philosophy (Read This Carefully)

## Retail thinks:
“Price reached resistance, so it should fall.”

## Institutions think:

* “Where can we accumulate or distribute without moving price too much, and where do we transfer risk to others?”

### True support & resistance = zones where positions were built, not where price turned.

### Important points

* Big money trades only where liquidity exists.


### Select stocks where at least 2 occur simultaneously:

1. Futures OI ↑ + Price ↑ (long buildup)

1. Futures OI ↑ + Price ↓ (short buildup)

1. Option chain showing fresh OI addition at specific strikes

1. Volume expansion without large price movement

- This narrows you to 3–5 stocks max → trade 2–3 best setups only.

📌 Rationale: Institutions cannot hide size. They reveal themselves through derivatives positioning before price moves.

--------------------------
### ✅ Institutional Support & Resistance = Positioning Zones

These zones come from 3 data layers, not visuals alone.

#### Layer-1 : FUTURE DATA
📌 True support forms where:

* OI increases

* Price stabilizes or slowly rises

* Volume contracts after expansion

📌 True resistance forms where:

* OI increases

* Price stalls or fades

* Aggressive volume spikes fail to push price

#### Layer-2 : OPTION DATA (WHERE THE RISK IS SOLD)
`Institutions sell options, they don’t buy lottery tickets.`

**Key Rules:**

* Highest Put OI zone → Institutional support

* Highest Call OI zone → Institutional resistance

* OI shifting strikes = changing bias

📌 If Put OI shifts UP with price → support is rising

📌 If Call OI adds aggressively on rise → upside capped

```Option writers defend zones until they don’t — when they exit, price moves FAST.```
